# Visual Identity Guidelines

## Colour Palette

- **Primary:** #0F0F0F (Deep Black)  
- **Secondary:** #FFFFFF (Pure White)  
- **Accent:** #A6A6A6 (Soft Steel Grey)  
- **Highlight:** #4A90E2 (Technical Blue)  

## Typography

- **Headings:** Inter, SemiBold  
- **Body:** Inter, Regular  
- **UI Labels:** Inter, Medium  

## Layout Rules

- Generous whitespace  
- Clean grid alignment  
- Minimal borders  
- High contrast  
- No decorative clutter  

## Imagery

- Large‑format previews  
- Clean room mockups  
- Neutral backgrounds  
- No busy or chaotic scenes
