# Copywriting Guidelines

## Product Descriptions

- Short, precise, premium  
- Focus on materials, scale, and craftsmanship  
- Avoid filler words  

## Collection Descriptions

- One paragraph max  
- Describe theme, mood, and visual identity  

## Microcopy

- Simple, direct actions  
- Examples: “Add to Cart”, “View Details”, “Generate Variation”  
- Avoid playful or quirky language  

## Email Tone

- Professional, warm, minimal  
- Clear value in every line
