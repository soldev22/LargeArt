# Brand Voice and Tone

## Voice Principles

- Clear, confident, modern  
- Minimalist language  
- Precision over poetry  
- Calm, premium, intentional  
- No hype, no clutter  

## Tone Guidelines

- Short, direct sentences  
- Avoid unnecessary adjectives  
- Speak with authority, not ego  
- Focus on clarity and usefulness  

## Do

- “Crafted with precision.”  
- “Engineered for large‑format impact.”  
- “Designed to elevate your space.”  

## Don’t

- “This is the most amazing art ever!”  
- “Super cool AI magic!”  
- Overly emotional or dramatic language
