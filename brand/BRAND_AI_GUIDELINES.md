# AI Generation Guidelines

## Art Style

- Modern, clean, premium  
- Avoid chaotic or overly abstract outputs  
- Maintain colour harmony  
- Prefer large‑format compositions  

## Prompt Structure

1. Subject  
2. Style direction  
3. Colour palette  
4. Composition notes  
5. Lighting  
6. Technical quality  

## Metadata Style

- Minimalist descriptions  
- Focus on mood, composition, and materials  
- Avoid dramatic or poetic language  

## Naming Conventions

- One or two‑word titles  
- Clean, modern, abstract names  
- Examples: “Horizon”, “Vector”, “Stillness”, “Flux”
